## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is Cyberia Space?**

A: Cyberia combines four views—Canvas, Directory, Kanban, and Calendar—on the same data. Switch between freeform canvases, list views, task boards, and timeline views instantly. Oracle AI acts on your content, not just answers questions. It's the only workspace where the AI doesn't just chat—it creates, edits, and organizes.

**Q: Is my data private and secure?**

A: Yes. Your space follows a Local-First philosophy. By default, all your work is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB. When you authenticate, data is synced securely via HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit and stored securely in the cloud.

**Q: How does Oracle AI handle my data?**

A: Oracle uses advanced reasoning models to analyze your space in real-time. We never use your personal data or content to train AI models. Your history stays local on your device.

**Q: What happens when I downgrade from Pro to Free?**

A: When you downgrade, nothing is deleted. All your thoughts, spaces, and files stay safe and accessible. You just won't be able to create beyond free plan limits until you upgrade again.

**Q: Can I export my data?**

A: Yes. Your data is yours. Export everything anytime in standard formats. No vendor lock-in.

**Q: Does Cyberia work offline?**

A: Yes. Cyberia is built offline-first. All your work is stored locally, so you can keep working without an internet connection. Changes sync automatically when you're back online.

**Q: What's the difference between Canvas and Directory?**

A: Canvas is for freeform, spatial thinking—arrange thoughts visually like notes on a desk. Directory is for structured organization—find anything instantly with search and filters.

**Q: How does Oracle AI differ from a chatbot?**

A: Most AI tools are just chat interfaces disconnected from your work. Oracle AI reads your files, understands your workspace context, creates tasks, edits content, and organizes—all while you focus on what matters.

**Q: Can I use Cyberia on mobile?**

A: Cyberia is currently optimized for desktop. Mobile companion app is coming soon.

**Q: Is my data used to train AI models?**

A: No. Your data is never used for training. Oracle AI uses advanced reasoning models with zero data retention policies.
